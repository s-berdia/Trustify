Privacy Policy for Trustify Chrome Extension Last updated: 10/27/2024 Introduction The Amazon Trustify Chrome Extension ("we", "our", "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension provides users with a Trust Score for Amazon products based on publicly available product information directly from Amazon’s website. Information We Collect The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, browsing history, or identifiable user data. All information used by the Extension, such as product names, prices, ratings, and shipping status, is publicly available on Amazon product listing pages and is processed locally on the user’s device. How We Use Information The Extension processes Amazon product information locally to calculate a Trust Score that aids consumers in making informed purchase decisions. No data is stored, sent to external servers, or shared with third parties. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Contact Us If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at https://github.com/s-berdia.
